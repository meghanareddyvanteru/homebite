About HomeBite

HomeBite is a platform that brings together passionate home chefs and food lovers. It enables chefs to share their homemade dishes and earn through their culinary skills, while customers can enjoy fresh, authentic meals right from the comfort of home.

Why HomeBite?

  • Support local chefs and home-based food businesses
  • Discover unique and authentic homemade dishes
  • Simple and secure ordering system

What We Offer

  • For Customers: Browse dishes, place orders, view order history
  • For Chefs: Upload dishes, manage orders, track earnings
  • Payment Simulation: Test payments safely during development

Our Mission

To empower home chefs and provide a platform where good food brings people together.
